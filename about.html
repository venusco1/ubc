Who We Are

About United Boards & Chem

Premium Plywoods in Kasaragod, Kerala

United Boards & Chem, based in Kasargod, Kerala, is dedicated to producing high-quality Ocuma and Gurjan plywood trusted across South India. We manufacture a wide range of variants — Semi-Commercial, Semi-Dipping, Alternate Commercial, and Alternate Dipping — each designed for strength, precision, and long-lasting performance.

Every sheet we make is built with carefully selected hardwood veneers and advanced bonding technology to ensure durability, smooth finish, and resistance to tough weather conditions. Our focus on innovation, consistency, and fine craftsmanship helps every customer achieve reliable results for years to come.

At United Boards & Chem, we believe in more than just manufacturing plywood — we build materials that form the foundation of homes, offices, and industries. From interior design to large-scale construction, our products are known for strength, reliability, and lasting beauty.

Discover our range of Ocuma and Gurjan-grade plywood and experience how our dedication to quality, technology, and customer satisfaction makes every sheet a symbol of trust and excellence.
