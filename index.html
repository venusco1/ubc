Who We Are

About United Boards & Chem

Premium Plywoods in Kasaragod, Kerala

Founded with a vision to deliver reliable, high-performance plywood, United Boards & Chem has become a trusted name for builders, contractors, and furniture makers across South India. Our Ocuma and Gurjan plywood ranges are crafted from carefully selected hardwood veneers and undergo strict quality checks, ensuring every sheet delivers unmatched strength, durability, and precision.

  • Top-Quality Materials

    We source premium Ocuma and Gurjan veneers and apply strict quality checks so every sheet delivers consistent strength and a clean finish.

  • Durable & Moisture-Resistant

    Advanced dipping and pressing processes enhance moisture resistance and dimensional stability for reliable performance in real-world conditions.
