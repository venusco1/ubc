Explore the Ocuma Plywood Range

United Boards & Chem offers a diverse range of Ocuma plywood products that combine strength, affordability, and durability. Each variant is crafted using high-quality Ocuma veneers and resins to deliver dependable performance for furniture, interiors, and architectural projects.

Ocuma Semi Commercial

An affordable yet durable plywood made with balanced Ocuma veneers and quality resins. It delivers reliable strength for daily-use furniture and interiors while maintaining a smooth finish.

  • Economical and durable construction
  • Smooth surface for laminates and paints
  • Good resistance to bending and cracking
  • Uniform thickness and stable bonding
  • Applications: Wardrobes, beds, tables, and residential furniture.

Ocuma Semi Dipping

Specially treated through dipping, this plywood gains enhanced resistance to termites, fungus, and moisture — offering long-lasting reliability in semi-humid conditions.

  • Anti-termite and anti-fungal dipped treatment
  • Moisture-protected and dimensionally stable
  • Extended lifespan compared to regular plywood
  • Smooth surface for decorative laminates
  • Applications: Kitchens, bathrooms, and semi-moist interiors.

Ocuma Alternate Commercial

Built using alternate core and panel structure, this plywood ensures excellent stability and strength — ideal for both residential and office interiors.

  • Balanced construction for improved stability
  • Smooth and fine surface finish
  • Ideal strength-to-cost ratio
  • Consistent performance under varying conditions
  • Applications: Office furniture, cupboards, panels, and partitions.

Ocuma Alternate Dipping

Engineered for maximum endurance, this plywood undergoes a full chemical dipping process to resist moisture, termites, and temperature variations.

  • Complete resin dipping for water protection
  • High bonding strength and load capacity
  • Stable performance over time
  • Ideal for humid or high-traffic areas
  • Applications: Kitchens, offices, commercial interiors, and semi-outdoor furniture.
