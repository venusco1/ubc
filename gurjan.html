Explore The Gurjan Plywood Range

At United Boards & Chem, we specialize in manufacturing high-quality Gurjan plywood, trusted for its strength, bonding, and long-lasting performance. Each sheet is made using premium Gurjan hardwood veneers, bonded with advanced resins, and pressed under high pressure to deliver superior dimensional stability, resistance, and finish. Our Gurjan plywood range is designed to meet diverse application needs — from furniture and interiors to high-moisture environments and commercial installations.

Gurjan Semi Commercial

Crafted with carefully selected Gurjan veneers, this plywood offers the perfect balance of strength and affordability. Designed for everyday furniture and general interior applications, it ensures reliable performance and a smooth finish.

  • Strong and durable for long-term use
  • Resistant to warping and cracking
  • Ideal for regular interior work and furniture
  • Smooth surface suitable for laminates and veneers
  • Applications: Used for wardrobes, cabinets, tables, and residential interiors.

Gurjan Semi Dipping

Surface-treated plywood offering enhanced protection against insects and damp conditions. Each sheet is resin-soaked to improve durability and longevity, making it ideal for humid environments.

  • Anti-fungal and termite-resistant treatment
  • Moisture and humidity protection
  • High screw-holding capacity
  • Stable even in variable weather conditions
  • Applications: Perfect for kitchens, bathrooms, and semi-damp interior zones.

Gurjan Alternate Commercial

Built with alternate core construction using premium hardwoods and Gurjan face veneers, this plywood combines structural stability with cost efficiency — perfect for residential and light commercial use.

  • Uniform strength and dimensional balance
  • Resistant to bending and delamination
  • Smooth surface suitable for polishing or lamination
  • Value-for-money solution for bulk projects
  • Applications: Office furniture, modular interiors, and partitions.

Gurjan Alternate Dipping

A fully treated, resin-dipped plywood designed for superior moisture resistance and mechanical strength. Ideal for environments demanding long-lasting performance.

  • Deep-dipping treatment ensures water and pest resistance
  • High bonding strength and stability under pressure
  • Extended lifespan under tough conditions
  • Retains strength and appearance over time
  • Applications: Kitchens, hotels, and heavy-duty commercial interiors.

Gurjan Full Red Commercial

Manufactured from 100% Gurjan core and face veneers, this premium-grade plywood offers exceptional strength, uniform color, and superior finish — setting the standard for quality plywood.

  • 100% Gurjan hardwood core construction
  • ISI-grade bonding with superior resin
  • Excellent load-bearing and screw-holding capacity
  • Elegant natural reddish tone finish
  • Applications: High-end furniture, interior panels, and luxury interiors.

Gurjan Full Red Dipping

The ultimate in durability and performance — made from 100% Gurjan veneers and specially dipped for water and termite resistance. Designed for long-term strength in demanding conditions.

  • 100% Gurjan hardwood composition
  • Resin-dipped and treated for water and termite resistance
  • Superior mechanical stability
  • Retains shape, texture, and polish for years
  • Applications: Premium interiors, modular kitchens, resorts, and humid commercial spaces.
